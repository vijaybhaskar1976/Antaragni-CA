# Content Specification & Copy Deck

This document serves as the absolute single source of truth for all typography, messaging, and copy required for the Antaragni Campus Ambassador website.

Developers must extract all strings directly from this document. **No placeholder text (Lorem Ipsum) or generic filler is permitted.**

---

## 1. BRAND VOICE

### Tone
Premium, challenging, exclusive, cinematic, and authoritative.
### Personality
Mysterious yet highly prestigious. It speaks like a luxury brand or an exclusive underground society, not a typical college festival.
### Vocabulary Style
Bold, punchy, unyielding. Short sentences. High-impact verbs.
### Writing Principles
1. Eliminate filler words.
2. Let the typography breathe—say more with less.
3. Frame the program as an exclusive privilege, not a desperate plea for marketing help.
### Words To Use
Legacy, Spirit, Represent, Exclusive, Privilege, Phenomenon.
### Words To Avoid
"Sign up now", "Join us today", "Click here", "Help us promote", "Free goodies".
